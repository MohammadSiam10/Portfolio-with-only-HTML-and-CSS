Dhuppani, Bilaicchori

On November 20, 2022, our group of six ventured out to explore the wonders of nature, capturing the enchanting beauty of Dhuppani Waterfall, Nokata Waterfall, and Muttochara Waterfall. Likhiyang

Bandarban

On October 18, 2023, an impromptu decision led eight of us on an unforgettable journey. Our destination? The fourth-largest waterfall in Bangladesh. This mesmerizing seasonal cascade proved to be one of the most beautiful natural wonders in the country. Spontaneity met awe-inspiring beauty, and our day was filled with the magic of discovery.

Rappelling & Jumaring

Khoiyachora, Mirashorai

June 24, 2023, marked an adventurous day as I indulged in rappelling and jumaring at Khoiyachora Waterfall. Nestled in the hills of Mirsharai, Chittagong, Bangladesh, this hilly cascade became the backdrop for a thrilling escapade, leaving me with memories of both the rush and serenity nature can offer.

Nafakhum

Remakri, Bandarban

On the 27th of May 2022, our journey led us to Nafakhum, the largest waterfall in Bangladesh in terms of water flow volume. Nestled on the Sangu River, its majestic beauty captivated us. Exploring the surrounding wonders, we visited Amiakhum (locally known as Hamiakhum), Satbhaikhum, and Vela Khum, completing a quartet of Khums. These natural treasures etched vivid memories of the breathtaking landscapes in our hearts.
